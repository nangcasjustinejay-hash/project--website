About Graham Balls

Graham Balls – Sweetness You Can’t Resist! Soft, chewy, and loaded with creamy goodness — our Graham Balls are the ultimate bite-sized happiness! Each ball is made with crushed graham crackers, milk, and a whole lot of love, then rolled and coated with yummy sprinkles or chocolate. One bite, and you’ll keep coming back for more.

Classic Graham Balls
